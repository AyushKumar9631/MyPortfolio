JEE Preparation

Vibrant Academy

Kota, Rajasthan
Drop Year Program  ·  2021 – 2022

Enrolled in Vibrant Academy's intensive drop-year JEE program — on the recommendation of a Don Bosco senior — this year in Kota proved to be far more than an academic exercise. Achieving a JEE Mains percentile of 97.1 secured admission to NIT Patna. But the deeper lesson learned here had nothing to do with scores.

Witnessing Professor Narendra Awasthi complete his second 10km lap of a marathon while I had not yet finished my first 5km was a moment of profound clarity. It redefined what persistence and a growth mindset truly look like when lived — not just taught.
