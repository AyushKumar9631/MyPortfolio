Full Stack FHIR R4 PWA Live on Vercel

VaidyaScribe

Ambient AI Clinical Scribe · A doctor speaks. A medical record is born.

An ambient AI clinical scribe built for Indian healthcare. A doctor records a consultation — VaidyaScribe transcribes via Groq Whisper, extracts 10 clinical entity types with Qwen3-32B, generates editable SOAP notes, and produces a standards-compliant FHIR R4 bundle — all in under 5 seconds. Nothing is saved until the doctor reviews and confirms all four outputs.

Live Demo GitHub
AI Pipeline
Doctor speaks ── Browser MediaRecorder ↓ Groq Whisper large-v3 (< 2s) ↓ raw transcript Qwen3-32B clinical NLP (< 3s) ↓ 10 structured entity types SOAP derivation + FHIR R4 build ↓ client-side, zero latency Doctor reviews → Save Record

Two hand-trained deep learning models — a CNN for binary cat/dog classification and a dense network for handwritten digit recognition — served entirely in the browser via TensorFlow.js. Upload any image and watch AI inference happen in real time, with zero server round-trips and no data ever leaving your device.
